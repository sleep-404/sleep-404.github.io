I did not ever expect that I'd fall for a girl whom I never even met 😂, but that's how it is now. A lot has happened during the 
past 4 months in my life and I'll try to pen down the same.

## First Contact
This dates back to February, where I met her on an online platform, yep... you guessed it wrong, not on some dating platform but rather
on a blood-thirsty & toxic game called ***Valorant***, how usual, right? I used to stay all day online just to play those 1-2 matches with her. Slowly from there we switched to *LinkedIn* because I was too much of a \*ussy to ask her for her number. But then thanks to a desperate guy, we formed a group on *WhatsApp* and that's how I got her number 😊 which I've been wanting to ask for a really looong time. Up until now, even though we used to talk.... it was not that much, but things kinda changed when switched to whatsapp.
<!-- --- -->


## Having a crush
As we started having more and more conversations, I kinda developed a crush towards her and have shared the same (indirectly), I tweeted about that and gave her my twitter username. That's when I came to know that she ain't single. She was super clear that the feelings cannot be reciprocated and it's upto me if I want to continue this friendship. Ofc it did not hurt, as I had no other intentions apart from having a good friend.... atleast that's what I thought.
<!-- --- -->


## Transformation of feelings
You know.... in life some of the best things happen without you asking for them. In my case, it was me falling for her 🙂. Even before I realised it, I was in love with her. I used to survive my day looking forward to playing game with her during the night. Every whatsapp ping was no less than a mini-present for me. I... who never bothered to use my phone, started checking it every 15-20 mins to see if I received a new message from her. Everytime I recieved a voice-note I used to float.
