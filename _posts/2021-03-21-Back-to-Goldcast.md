## Interview call from Kivi Capital !
I applied to Kivi Capital on Instahyre, and to my surprise my Instahyre score was FULL, like the bar was complete
indicating a very high match. A little context, Kivi Capital is a HFT firm, that pays ridiculously high, more than
Google, Goldmansachs and definitely more than any other startup in India. So the CEO himself reached out to schedule
an interview. At first I just agreed to whatever date he mentioned, which gave about 6 days time to prepare. Later
that night after going through the job description, although I had every mentioned point that I am good at, I just felt 
that I ain't my best at the moment. So here comes the gamble! The very next morning I mailed him regarding the same
and said it would just be a waste of time if he interviwed me right now, and asked him would he do the same 3 months
from now if the job opening still exists ? I don't know, not only my future interview isn't guranteed but I'm also
risking my current interview. And luckily he agreed to it, assuring that my application will be taken forward directly
in the month of July if the opening still exists.  
Now why is this a big thing ? Because Kivi Capital wasn't even open to my department (Aerospace) when it visited our campus during placements,
and to top it off only single digit students were selected for interviews among CS/EE students.  

That being said, there are also few other firms that I'll be interviewing with for now. I guess acche din started!
