I've been wanting to write this down for a while. So last week, midst of chaos I've been listening to
[this playlist](https://open.spotify.com/playlist/6KHBPAYBG4S6aftlgAOZBC?si=68f1998590464ec0) in 
loop, at the same time I was also stalking (of course harmless) this girl while playing valorant with her.
That's when these strange, new feelings started to appear. Look for a guy who spent most of his childhood 
in a cave, unable to speak to girls, with **0** girl contacts in phonebook, it happens. My advise to people
like me or to the future me, don't ever listen to songs and talk to girls at the same time. Your stupid brain
starts thinking that the songs are made just for you and even the girl on the other side feels the same. In reality,
nobody gives a crap about what you are feeling, unless they are really close/someone you already know, and that 
definitely ain't the girl you met online on some platform. 

## That Urge to talk to her
After wrapping up with my assignments, I started staying online all day so that I wouldn't miss a chance to
play with her. Sometimes she would start the match even without inviting, ouch that hurts. One day, during a
convo with her on LinkedIn I tried asking her if we can connect on any other social media platform she is active
on, this bucking LinkedIn UI doesn't show when someone is typing, if a message is seen or not, etc and I wanted
to convey her the same, but ended up getting her insta_id (pro-stalk mode: ON). Though it would've been nice had
I gotten her number or became friends on FB. now that I have her insta id, I kinda swiped through all photos, 
and read few comments. That's when I saw she has 1k followers, da faq !! Even if I add all my friends on facebook,
twitter, linkedIn I still couldn't cross that. Everyday I wanted to spend little more time with her fearing starting
next week that might not be possible. It legit feels nice talking to a girl on any random topic.

## And then this happens....
Just when I thought things were favoring me, this happens, I was being addressed by a girl by that one word which,
I guess every guy in the world hates being called, ***BRO***. Come one I already have enough sisters, don't want 
anymore. I got to hear her yesterday night, and DAMN ! her voice is cute. She was ranting on about an A*shole who 
was an A*shole to her during one of her game. I still don't get it, how come people be rude to girls, like any girl,
let alone curse them, I cannot even speak to them. And right after receiving that awesome voice note, three nuclear
bombs, "bro", "bro" and "bro" were dropped. Dang, I know that not everyone feels the same about eachother, I know that
it is foolish to expect someone to be more friendly whom you have only met, I know that.... but it still is hard to 
accept it. Even the wait after a DM feels like eternity, and of course it is long, about 3 hrs. I know all these clearly
show that she ain't interested in me, but honestly, I had no control on myself. I clearly was not being myself. Finally after
participating in Codechef, Codeforces and Google's Kickstart I got a reality check.

## Inshort
Basically I am no match for someone with 1k followers on insta, and that being said, I also am not in a position where one
might be proud of me. As much as I want to talk to someone, share my thoughts, goals, I also want the other person to be
atleast slightly interested in me, being shut on face is one sign that helps you decide fast if one's interested in you or not.
After completly screwing yesterdays competitons, I decided to write a blog at the end of the day, everyday, regarding things I learnt,
and an unofficial one, regarding what new feelings I discovered in me.
