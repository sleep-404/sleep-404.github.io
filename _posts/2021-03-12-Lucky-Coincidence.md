## How I met a new friend
It was yet another normal day with me procrastinating my prepartion.... yep I was playing that damn
game called Valorant. But that day something nice happened, a certain energetic kid kept on screaming 
to revive him (my game character can't do it) and that's when few legendary words which go something 
like "*suck my balls*" came out of my mouth. Moments later, I hear another calm voice asking me to be
a little gentle as he is her brother and is only 10 years old (I honestly didn't expect him to be so young).
After that we became game friends.

## Back to my senses
Right when I thought I got a new friend, had to go out of city for 6 days. When back (March 1), I was super excited
to play valorant with them again. I would come online everyday before lunch and stay online waiting for them, sometimes
they would join, sometimes not. One such day, I came to know from her brother that she couldn't join as she is studying
for her exams. Now, that hit me hard !. She graduated already and has a job, yet she is preparing and working hard. Me
on the other hand, still being a student with no job am wasting my time here. I always had that container of motivation +
hardwork full, it just required a few more drops to overcome that potential barrier, and this time I got it in litres.
